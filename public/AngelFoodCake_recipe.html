Add your oil to a pan, frying the garlic first! After 3 minutes of frying, place your onions and carrots in as well! Add in your rice after another 3 minutes! Crack an egg, stirring and tossing it into the rice. Add your salt, pepper, and soy sauce! Stir your rice constantly for 3 minutes. This recipe is quick and easy to make. It's the best dish to make when you're feeling down, bored, hungry, or if you simply don't have enough time to whip up anything else! It's a nutritious meal that I make A LOT as a student who doesn't really have enough time to make bigger meals. It honestly just takes as long as heating up instant noodles! Optionally, you could add bits of meat while cooking this to get in some protein! Enjoyyy!!!
